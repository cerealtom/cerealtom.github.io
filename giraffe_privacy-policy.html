Privacy policy

The Bright Yellow Giraffe app doesn't collect user data, and as such, no user data will be stored or used in any way, because it doesn't exist. Nor does this website use cookies. The Bright Yellow Giraffe is a picture book app for kids, and Google requires any app whose target audience is under 13 years of age to provide a privacy policy. This is that.

Users cannot in any way interact or exchange information, this app does not share user-provided personal information with third parties, and this app does not share any user's physical location with other users.
