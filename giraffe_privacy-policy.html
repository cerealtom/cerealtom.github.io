Privacy policy

The Bright Yellow Giraffe app doesn't collect user data, and as such, no user data will be stored or used in any way, because it doesn't exist. The Bright Yellow Giraffe is a picture book app for kids, and Google requires any app whose target audience is under 13 years of age to provide a privacy policy. This is that.

The Google Play Store may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, and information about the way you use the Application.

Users cannot in any way interact or exchange information, the app does not share user-provided personal information with third parties, and the app does not share any user's physical location with other users.
